ABOUT

"Lens Poetry", a captivating journey through the world of minimalistic street style and nature photography. Lens Poetry is my creative canvas where I share my passion for mobile camera photography. Through my lens, I strive to capture the world's quiet elegance and the unspoken stories within it. From the simplicity of vintage streets to the tranquil beauty of nature, each image is a visual poem, revealing the artistry in simplicity. It is an invitation to see the extraordinary in the ordinary, a testament to the power of a single frame. It's my journey of exploring life's subtleties and sharing them with you. Join me in this visual poetry adventure.

I'm Tushar Pal, a small-town dweller from a charming village in West Bengal, India. While my days are filled with engineering studies, my heart belongs to the world of art. As an amateur painter, I bring my creativity to life on canvas. Photography, however, is where I find serenity and capture the beauty of the everyday. Through my lens, I transform moments into memories, revealing the hidden elegance in simplicity. Join me on my photographic journey, where I strive to share the world as I see it, one frame at a time. Welcome to my photography page; let's explore life's beauty together.

MY WORKS

My mobile camera photographs are windows to a world of simplicity and elegance. I uncover the poetry in everyday moments, unveiling hidden beauty. Each image is a visual story, capturing emotions, nature's wonders, and the grace of urban life. Through these photos, I aim to share the enchantment I find in life's subtleties and inspire a deeper appreciation for the world's ordinary splendors.
